Whether Josh Hazlewood, who is waiting to be fit, will be there, was in doubt. Finally, Australia has announced the final team of the World Test Championship with this pacer. India will be Australia’s opponent in the final, which will begin on June 7 at the Oval.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Pacer Hazlewood and all-rounder Mitchell Marsh \u2014 both withdrew from the IPL early with injuries. There was also talk that Hazlewood would be replaced by Michael Nesser. Hazlewood is finally in the main team. Cricket Australia gave the final 15-man squad to the ICC on Sunday. Marsh has been kept as standby. Matthew Renshaw also went to the standby list with Marsh from the 17-man squad.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Nesser and Shane Abbott will assist the Australian team in training. Wicketkeeper Josh Inglis has been kept as backup to Alex Carey in the main team. Todd Murphy is the spinner along with Nathan Lyon.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Meanwhile, India did not make any changes in the original team of 15 people. However, Yasaswi Jaiswal has been brought in place of Ruturaj Gaikwad who is on standby. It is known that Gaikwad has removed himself because he will get married.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Jaiswal has had a great season for Rajasthan Royals in the IPL. He scored 625 runs in 14 matches at a strike rate of 163.61. Suryakumar Yadav and Mukesh Kumar are on standby with Jaiswal.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Those whose teams were earlier eliminated from the IPL have already gone to England to start training. Virat Kohli is also in this team.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

This is going to be the second final of the World Test Championship. India lost to New Zealand in the final of the last season.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Team Australia:
Pat Cummins (captain), Scott Boland, Alex Carey (wicketkeeper), Cameron Greene, Marcus Harris, Josh Hazlewood, Travis Head, Josh Inglis (wicketkeeper), Usman Khawaja, Marnus Labuschagne, Nathan Lyon, Todd Murphy, Steve Smith (Vice-Captain), Mitchell Starc, David Warner.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Standby: Mitchell Marsh, Matthew Renshaw.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Team India:
Rohit Sharma (captain), Shubman Gill, Cheteshwar Pujara, Virat Kohli, Ajinkya Rahane, Srikar Bharat (wicketkeeper), Ravichandran Ashwin, Ravindra Jadeja, Akshar Patel, Shardul Thakur, Mohammad Shami, Mohammad Siraj, Umesh Yadav, Jaydev Unadkat , Ishan Kishan (wicketkeeper).<\/p>\n\n\n\n
