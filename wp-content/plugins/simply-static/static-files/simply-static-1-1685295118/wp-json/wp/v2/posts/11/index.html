Recep Tayyip Erdogan has been elected for the third consecutive term as the President of Turkey. He won the second round of the presidential election held in the country on Sunday.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Turkey’s state news agency Anadolu reported that 97 percent of the votes had been counted. Erdo\u011fan got 52.1 percent of the votes. And the candidate of the main opposition alliance, Kemal Kilicdarolu, got 47.9 percent of the votes. Erdo\u011fan was re-elected as the private president.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

National elections were held in Turkey on May 14. At that time, no presidential candidate received 50 percent of the vote. Erdogan came closest to winning with 49.5 percent of the vote. Next was Kemal Kilicdarolu. His vote was 44.8 percent. As a result, the vote was conducted in the second round.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
